When you text our business phone number, we collect your mobile phone number and the content of your messages, in order to respond to inquiries, schedule appointments, and send appointment confirmations and reminders.

We use your phone number and message content solely to communicate with you about appointments, services, pricing, and related customer service matters. We do not use this information for any other purpose.

By texting our business number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to appointment scheduling, confirmations, and reminders. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us.

No Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or message content with third parties for marketing purposes. Mobile opt-in information and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except as required to provide the messaging service itself (e.g., our SMS service provider).

Opting Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You may reply HELP for assistance. For more information, see our Terms and Conditions.

Data Retention

We retain message and appointment history only as long as needed to provide our services and comply with applicable law.
